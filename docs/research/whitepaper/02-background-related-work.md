## Motivation

Lyme surveillance data are useful for public context, but reported cases lag
exposure and reflect reporting systems, case definitions, clinical behavior,
and source coverage. TickBiteRisk treats reported incidence as a proxy for
relative pressure, not as a complete measure of infections.

## Source Caveats
