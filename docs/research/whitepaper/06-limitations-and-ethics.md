# Limitations And Ethics

Draft status: working draft derived from internal lab notes; not release-ready.

Internal evidence record: docs/research/lab-notes

## Overclaim Risks

The main communication risks are treating reported surveillance as complete
disease measurement, treating weekly seasonal allocation as observed weekly
truth, treating exploratory branches as public defaults, or treating display
scores as clinical guidance.

## Medical Boundary

TickBiteRisk may provide informational context and link to official guidance,
but medical decisions belong to qualified healthcare professionals and public
health authorities. Product language must not override clinician judgment.

## Data Gaps

Known gaps include absent observed county-week Lyme outcomes, uneven regional
source regimes, provisional overlays, denominator timing issues, ecology
candidate uncertainty, and incomplete bibliography reconstruction.

## Human Review Gates

Human-in-the-loop review is required before changing public branch selection,
expanding regional public claims, changing medical wording, publishing a
release-ready whitepaper, or redistributing raw or terms-unclear source data.
