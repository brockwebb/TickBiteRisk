# Results And Validation

Draft status: working draft derived from internal lab notes; not release-ready.

Internal evidence record: docs/research/lab-notes

## Rolling-Origin Validation

Rolling-origin validation is the main evidence gate for forecast readiness.
Validation compares county-year forecasts with later reported annual outcomes
at the same temporal grain.

## Current Public Branch

The Maryland public surface remains tied to the selected public branch recorded
in model cards and source catalogs. Research branches may be inspected beside
it, but they do not replace it without review.

## Calibration And Bayesian Gates

Current calibration and Gamma-Poisson Bayesian update backtests are research
materials. Their overall gates should remain controlling for public use, even
when subgroup diagnostics suggest future design ideas.

## Regional Diagnostics

Regional diagnostics support stress testing of spatial context, intervals,
typicality language, and source coverage. They are not the Maryland public
default and should not be promoted as a public regional product without
human-in-the-loop approval.
